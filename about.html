About Awakening Insurance Agency LLC

A nationally operating insurance agency built on transparency, trust, and a genuine commitment to protecting what matters most to you.

Our Mission

Insurance That Works for People, Not Against Them

Awakening Insurance Agency LLC was founded with a clear purpose: to bring clarity, fairness and genuine human care to an industry that too often feels complicated and impersonal.

We operate nationwide, serving individual drivers, families, business owners and fleet operators with the same level of dedication. Whether you need coverage for one vehicle or five hundred, we approach every client with the same commitment to finding the best protection at the best price.

Our licensed advisors average over 12 years of industry experience. We are not just policy sellers — we are your long-term protection partner.
